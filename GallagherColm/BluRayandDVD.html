Services

Satellite Services

Satellite installations is one of the most popular services that we offer. We offer satellite installations which cover all UK and Irish channels and we can also install satellites that offer channels from many different parts of the world too.

All of our satellite installations are fitted by trained, qualified professionals who have the equipment and knowledge that is needed to get the best satellite signal from whatever station you require.

When people think of Satellite Television they usually think of the big provider beaming UK pay television into Ireland, but there are many other Satellites beaming television from many countries across the globe, all of which can be recieved here in Ireland. At Shane Kelly Communications we can install them all, no matter what part of the world you come from we can most likely get TV channels from your home country. We use specialist equipment capable of measuring signal strength, picture quality, VBER MER and signal to noise ratio. Don't worry if you don't know what this means it just means we do the job right.

We work with all domestic, commercial and industrial properties. We regularly re-train so we can continue to be the best at what we do.

Customer satisfaction is our main goal at Shane Kelly Communications, so instead of just installing a satellite dish, taking your money and leaving, we are always cautious and think about what is best for the customer. Not only that, our prices remain amongst the most competitive.

TV Services

If you’re looking for a reliable TV aerial installation service in Kerry, then look no further than Shane Kelly Communications. We operate in Kerry and across Munster, and pride ourselves on offering a fast and reliable aerial installation service at a price that’s right.

Perhaps your TV aerial is old and is having an adverse effect on the quality of your reception. It may have been damaged in a recent storm, or the cable sleeve may be worn through. There are lots of reasons why your TV aerial, or its connecting cable may be causing problems with your television reception. This is where Shane Kelly Communications can help in Kerry. We will quickly identify the problem and do a complete repair or replacement on the spot.

Sometimes, problems can be caused by ‘too much’ signal. All often people buy the highest gain aerial they can get their hands on, when the reality is they’re too close to the transmitter for high gain to be of benefit. In fact, it can also have an adverse effect on your reception. Using our specialist monitoring equipment, we will install a TV aerial which is perfectly calibrated to the transmitted signal in your area, giving you a crisp and clear reception in Kerry.

Who we are

Shane Kelly Communications is a Kerry based company and can help you with all aspects of Satellite Installations, Television, Satellites,Radio, DVD/Home Cinema, Plasma Screens and just about anything else to do with television. Based in Lyreacrompane we cover most of Munster. We can usually provide a same day service in the Kerry region, and the rest of Munster on a next day basis.

Every installation includes an obligation-free site survey. We also supply an on-the-spot quotation or, depending on your area, a quotation can be arranged over the phone or via email.

We are members of the Registered Digital Installers Licensing Body (RDI) and the Confederation of Aerial Industries (CAI). The CAI is Ireland's only official body to register licensed digital aerial and TV systems installers. The scheme is backed by the Government and requires every member to become qualified and undergo important checks.

Only professionally trained installers like us who are members of the RDI and CAI can meet these demands!

Not only do we supply the products you want, but we will come to your house, install it and make sure everything is set up properly AND show you how to use it all.
